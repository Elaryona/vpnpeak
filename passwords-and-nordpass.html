Passwords & NordPass: why a VPN is not enough

Your VPN hides your traffic – a password manager protects your accounts.

Most real‑world account takeovers don’t happen because someone “hacked the VPN”. They happen because passwords are weak, reused or leaked in data breaches. That’s why a password manager is the natural companion to a VPN.
